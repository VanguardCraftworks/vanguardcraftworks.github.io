Ordering & Translation Decisions

The Torah is the one volume where nearly every canonical tradition agrees. Genesis through Deuteronomy appears first in the Hebrew Bible, the Septuagint, the Latin Vulgate, and every major Protestant and Catholic Bible in print today. There is no controversy here — only foundation.

The translation used throughout is the New Living Translation (NLT). Permission has been requested from Tyndale House Publishers and this project is being documented publicly as it develops.
